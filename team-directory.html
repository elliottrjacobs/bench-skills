Department

Discovery & Strategy

The intelligence wing. They figure out who your customer is, how to position your product, what to charge, and how to reach the market. Start here when you're building something new.

Department

Product

From fuzzy idea to formalized spec. They brainstorm, name things, write PRDs, and produce technical specifications that engineering can execute against.

Department

Engineering

The builders. They plan implementations, execute code, review quality, and audit security. They turn specs into shipped software.

Department

Design

Brand strategy, creative direction, and design systems. They set the visual and strategic tone, then systematize it into reusable components.

Department

Marketing & Sales

Revenue generation. They write content, build landing pages, craft sales pitches, and close deals with proposals. The front line with customers.

Department

Operations

The team that keeps the team running. Onboards new projects, discovers new capabilities, documents institutional knowledge, and makes everything visible.

Handoff Matrix

Who feeds work to whom. The typical chain of command for getting things done.

From To What Gets Passed
/onboarding /client-discovery Project context file (projects/<name>/onboarding.md)
/client-discovery /positioning JTBD analysis, opportunity map, customer segments
/positioning /pricing-packaging Competitive frame, value proposition, category
/positioning /studio-brand-strategist Strategic narrative, differentiation, positioning statement
/pricing-packaging /gtm-strategist Pricing model, packaging tiers, monetization strategy
/product-brainstorm /product-prd Explored requirements, user stories, constraints
/product-prd /product-tech-spec Product requirements document
/product-tech-spec /engineer-plan Technical specification with architecture decisions
/engineer-plan /engineer-plan-review Implementation plan with task breakdown
/engineer-plan-review /engineer-work Simplified, approved plan
/engineer-work /engineer-review Branch with implemented code, PR
/engineer-review /security-audit Reviewed code for deep security analysis
/studio-brand-strategist /design-lead Brand voice, messaging framework, visual direction
/design-lead /design-system Design tenets, direction, quality standards
/gtm-strategist /content-writer Channel strategy, messaging, launch sequence
/gtm-strategist /sales-pitch Target segments, value props, competitive positioning
/content-writer /landing-page Marketing copy, value propositions, CTAs
/sales-pitch /proposal-writer Sales narrative, objection handling, pricing
Any skill /knowledge-compound Solutions, decisions, and learnings to document
